About Us

The Frame Interpolation Sketchpad is a simple web-based tool for creating hand-drawn animation frames and experimenting with frame interpolation techniques. Our goal is to make animation accessible and fun for everyone, from beginners to professionals.

This project was created by Faris Faizal and Dinesh Satyavolu.

For feedback or contributions, please visit our GitHub page.
